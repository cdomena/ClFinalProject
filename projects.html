Projects I've worked on

Over the past few years I've dabbled in different art mediums and all for fun. I started with sketch and over time decided that it would be awesome to actually have a figure of the characters I drew. So I tried sculpting in clay but just didnt feel comfortable and finally found a wax/clay hybrid called Castilene. No need for an armature and can hold incredible detail.
